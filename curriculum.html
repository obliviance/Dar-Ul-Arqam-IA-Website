Curriculum

Methods of articulation
Arabic is a very vocal language so in order to acheive fluency, children must be taught to properly vocalise every letter of the Arabic alphabet. Children learn to read by reading aloud, and it is for this reason that methods of articulation are so important.

Punctuation
Punctuation is also very important in the Arabic language and is particularly essential when learning to read or write. The Arabic language has a diverse set of punctuation that are all utilized in different ways.

Grammatical Rules
Grammatical Rules are taught to those students who mastered punctuation and methods of articulation of the Arabic language. Higher level grammatical rules are then taught to avoid confusion for younger students.
