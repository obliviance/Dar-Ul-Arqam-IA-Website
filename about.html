About

What is Dar-Ul-Arqam?

Dar-Ul-Arqam is a local Arabic school run by Aliya Mahdi that has been going on for over 10 years now. Dar-Ul-Arqam teaches kids ages 15 and younger how to read and write in Arabic, and does so using a combination of discipline, practice, and fun. Dar-Ul-Arqam allows children to pick up another language, and in todays fast-moving world, children need the best head start they can get.

Who is Aliya Mahdi?

Aliya has taught children arabic for over 14 years. She has studied arabic at many different institutions like Izzah and many different people like Farhat Hashimi. Aliya has also completed many courses and has been awarded many certificates. She has also completed her Teachers degree as of 2015, and is a kindergarten teacher during the week. Alia is also a dedicated and loving wife and mother of 4. She also teaches an older women's class at the SNMC mosque. Aliya has been owner and teacher at Dar-Ul-Arqam for over 10 years. Throughout those years she has enthusiastically taught children Arabic using new techniques and interesting activities to keep children emgaged. For more information, check out the Curriculum.
